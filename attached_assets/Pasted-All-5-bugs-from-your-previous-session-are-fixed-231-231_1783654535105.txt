## Strategic Implementation Notes
Before you pull the trigger on Phase 0, keep two micro-optimizations in mind to ensure the test suite remains deterministic and performant:
### 1. Isolating the Traffic Control (tc) Shaper
In Phase 7.3, when applying tc inside the containers to simulate latency and packet loss, ensure the network shaping rules are targeted **strictly to the libp2p P2P ports (e.g., Gossipsub/Kademlia traffic)** and *not* the loopback or internal bridge connections to each node's respective PostgreSQL container.
