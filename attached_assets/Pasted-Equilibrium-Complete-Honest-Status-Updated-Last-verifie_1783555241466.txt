The Philosophical Core

Verification vs Consensus

· Verification = the work itself. 178 tests passing. Determinism harness. 149 TPS. Contracts deployed and verified on‑chain. The code, running, producing truth you can read directly.
· Consensus = what other people say about the work. An audit is formalised consensus — a third party agreeing that the work checks out.

Bitcoin launched with verification and zero consensus. The consensus formed over years as people saw the verification hold.

Equilibrium already has the verification. The audit would add consensus — useful for community confidence, but not required for mainnet.
